I have this most days for breakfast with oatmeal . ... I like the texture of omelets a lot--the layer it creates and the filling (optional) inside.

Some ideas for omelet fillings: mushrooms, onions, spinach (THE BEST COMBO)

Sometime I have it without filling and fold the two edge thirds inwards to create more layers !! And that is good as well.

I remember first having a really good omelet in Chinatown, NYC at a Hong Kong Cafe and it is pretty replicable but theirs had cheddar cheese and mushrooms.

Ingredients:

3 Eggs, Avocado Oil, Salt, Onion (1/2 small), Mushroom (I like 2-3 baby bella mushrooms), Baby Spinach

Instructions:

I use an 11 inch nonstick pan. Wash the mushrooms and spinach. Oil the pan. Cut half a small onion thinly add salt and sautee for about 5 minutes then add sliced mushrooms. Sautee for another 5 minutes and add a bit of water if necessary. Remove from pan. Reoil the pan. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and pour. Add the mushroom, onion and fresh baby spinach. Then fold in half. Eat immediately.
