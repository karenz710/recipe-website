Ingredients:

Wild Alaskan Sockeye Salmon, Avocado Oil (other high temp), Salt, Quinoa, Kale, Apple, any Cheese, Dijon Mustard or other dressing

Instructions:

Cook quinoa. Scale the salmon if necessary. Salt liberally on both sides because we're not salting the kale or quinoa. Wait 15 minutes for moisture and the salmon temp to come down. Prep the apple cutting to cube and cut cheese to cubes. Pat down salmon and heat generous oil to medium. Wait till the pan is hot and then put the salmon skin side down. Be sure to press the salmon down with some weight because in the heat it curls and we want the entire skin to be crispy. Cooking time depends on the thickness but around 6 minutes or color up 2/3's of the way. We want to cook the salmon skin side down for most of the process to get crispyness. Only flip once and cook for 2 mins or so more till fully cooked. Place kale on the bottom a lot. And quinoa and the salmon, apple, cheese and dijon mustard.
