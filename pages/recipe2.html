I have this most days for breakfast. ... I mean practically everyday since high school because it is convenient, affordable, and makes me feel satiated. I have always been a fan of Bob's Red Mill Steel Cut Oats but I like rolled oats as well.

Currently I like my oatmeal with frozen wild blueberries from Trader Joeâ€™s for breakfast. I add about 1/4 cup wild blueberries. Other frozen berries are good as well but the small blueberries defrost thoroughly in the hot oatmeal.

Other ideas include banana and cocoa powder.

I reheat oatmeal adding a bit of water and microwaving, it stores in the fridge for 3-5 days.

My breakfast everyday is oatmeal and an omelet! Omelet for the protein, although the oatmeal itself has about 6 grams of protein. Link to OMELET RECIPE !!

Ingredients:

Steel-cut-oats, Water

Instructions:

I make 1 dry cup at once and eat 1/3 cup per serving ~ 230 cals for Bob's Red Mill. Add 3 water to 1 oats, so 3 cups of water for 1 cup dry oats. Boil and turn to low medium heat uncovered for 15-25 minutes.
